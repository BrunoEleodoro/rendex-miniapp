Transfer
You have received your credentials, and now weʼll walk through a complete use case that
expands upon the basic PIX to STABLE and then after on-chain conversions and transfers flow:
Use Case: PIX to BRLA → USDC/USDT → Subaccounts → External Wallets
This flow starts with a deposit in local currency via PIX to the BRLA stablecoin. From there,
BRLA is exchanged for another stable asset such as USDC or USDT.
Once the stable assets are available, subaccounts can be created to organize and segregate
balances. Funds are then transferred from the main account to the respective subaccounts as
needed.
Finally, tokens held in subaccounts can be transferred to external wallets.
